‚ú® Step 5: Complete Your First Mystery Shops

‚è∞ Time Required: 2-4 hours for first few shops

üéØ Your First Shop Success Strategy

Your first few shops set the foundation for your mystery shopping career. Focus on following instructions precisely, taking detailed notes, and submitting reports early to build credibility with companies.

1

Choosing Your First Shops

Start simple: Choose basic retail or restaurant visits

Pick nearby locations: Minimize travel time while learning

Low-stress environments: Avoid complex scenarios initially

Clear instructions: Choose shops with straightforward requirements

2

Pre-Shop Preparation

Read instructions 3 times: Understanding every requirement

Note key details: Employee names, specific questions to ask

Plan your route: Optimal timing and parking

Prepare materials: Payment method, note-taking tools

3

During the Shop

Act naturally: Be a typical customer, not suspicious

Observe everything: Cleanliness, staff behavior, wait times

Follow the scenario: Stick to provided scripts exactly

Mental notes first: Write details immediately after leaving

4

Post-Shop Report Writing

Write immediately: Complete report within 2-4 hours

Be specific: Use exact quotes and detailed descriptions

Answer all questions: Never leave blanks or say "N/A"

Proofread carefully: Check grammar, spelling, and accuracy

üèÜ First Shop Success Checklist

Read and understood all shop requirements
Planned route and timing for optimal experience
Completed shop following all scenario requirements
Took detailed notes immediately after visit
Submitted complete report within deadline
Saved all receipts and documentation
