ENHANCED EARNINGS STRATEGY READ THIS
  1. Priority System: Always tackle $20+/hour tasks first when your energy and focus are peak. Save easy tasks for low-energy periods.
  2. Peak Hours Strategy: Work during 9 AM - 2 PM when research studies and high-paying tasks are most available.
  3. Application Blitz: Apply to 10+ studies daily on Respondent and User Interviews. Most people only apply to 1-2.
  4. Platform Specialization: Pick 2-3 platforms and become really good at them rather than spreading thin across many.
  5. Compound Earnings: Use passive income apps while doing active tasks to create double income streams.
  6. Quality Over Quantity: One $50 study beats ten $2 surveys. Always prioritize higher-value opportunities.
