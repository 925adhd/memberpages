Payment timeframes vary by site. Some quick‑pay sites process within 48–72 hours, while others may take 1–2 weeks but typically offer higher rates. Each site's honest review tells expected payout timeframes.
What skills do I need?
None required. In the overview of each site, we cover requirements, time to first payout, and difficulty level. We have options for complete beginners to experienced professionals.
Is this passive income?
No. These are active tasks with clear steps and honest time expectations. While we do share some apps that work in the background after setup, we focus on legitimate work opportunities, not get‑rich‑quick schemes.
Will this work with my ADHD?
Yes—designed specifically for ADHD brains with short task sessions, single‑focus layouts, and "Do‑This‑Next" checklists. No overwhelming dashboards or decision paralysis.
Is it safe to give you my card info? How do I know this isn't a scam?
Three facts: (1) I never touch your payment info — Stripe handles everything (same processor as Netflix, Uber, Amazon). (2) You get instant access, no subscriptions, no recurring charges. (3) 30‑day money‑back guarantee — if you don't make money, you get your money back. Zero risk on your end.
What does "lifetime access" mean?
Lifetime access to the content and site directory. If our hosting service ever ends, you'll get an instant downloadable offline version plus CSV export of all site data.
How do you verify sites?
Each site must meet our 7‑point checklist: real payouts to us personally, clear terms, reliable payment processing, reasonable minimum cashout, active user base, responsive support, and legal compliance.
Is there an age restriction for your app or the jobs listed?
The 925 ADHD app itself has no age restrictions—it's simply a curated list of legitimate sites. Each site has its own age rules: many require 18+, but lower‑paying sites (especially gift card ones) often allow younger teens. Always check each site's Terms of Service or contact their support to confirm age requirements.
