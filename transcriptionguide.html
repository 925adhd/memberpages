🤖 AI Impact: The Real Story

Rather than eliminating all human roles, AI has shifted what transcriptionists do and how work is structured. Your ADHD brain brings unique value to AI collaboration!

📊 AI Performance vs Human Strengths

What AI does well (90%+ accuracy):

  • Clear audio with standard accents
  • 5-minute turnaround for 30-minute files

Where AI still struggles (your opportunity):

  • Heavy accents or technical jargon
  • Poor audio quality or background noise
  • Context-dependent homophones
  • Emotional nuance and speaker identification

⚡ ADHD-Specific Success Strategies

🎯 The "Energy-Matching" Workflow System

Work WITH your ADHD brain rather than against it. Match tasks to your current state:

⚡

High Energy/Focus Days

  • Tackle difficult audio with multiple speakers
  • Handle technical or specialized content
  • Take on longer files (30+ minutes)
  • Work on higher-paying, complex projects
🔋

Medium Energy Days

  • Standard single-speaker transcription
  • Routine AI editing tasks
  • Files in the 10-20 minute range
  • Familiar content types you've done before
🪫

Low Energy Days

  • Simple proofreading of existing transcripts
  • Very clear, high-quality audio files
  • Short files under 10 minutes
  • Platform training or guideline review
😴

No Energy Days

  • Skip transcription entirely - don't force it!
  • Use time for skill development or research
  • Rest and recharge rather than poor-quality work

🧠 Managing ADHD-Specific Challenges

1

Perfectionism Traps

Challenge: Getting stuck replaying unclear audio endlessly.

Strategy: Set 2-minute limit per unclear section. Flag for review, add timestamps, move on.

2

Time Blindness

Challenge: Drastically underestimating file completion time.

Strategy: Track actual vs estimated time for 10-20 files. Add 50% buffer to estimates.

3

Motivation Fluctuations

Challenge: Inconsistent productivity affecting earnings.

Strategy: Create backup task lists for low-motivation periods. Visual progress tracking.

4

Sustained Attention Fatigue

Challenge: Long files becoming overwhelming.

Strategy: Break into 10-15 minute segments. Use Pomodoro technique.

5

Hyperfocus Management

Challenge: Working hours without breaks, leading to burnout.

Strategy: Set alarms for breaks. Quality-check work every hour. Protect your health!

🏠 ADHD-Optimized Workspace

Sensory considerations:

  • Lighting: Warm, adjustable desk lighting (avoid harsh overhead)
  • Noise Control: Noise-cancelling headphones even when not transcribing
  • Fidget Tools: Stress ball, fidget cube for restless energy
  • Temperature: Keep slightly cool to maintain alertness

Organization systems:

  • Visual Tracking: Color-coded folders for platforms/clients
  • Progress Displays: Visible charts showing daily/weekly goals
  • Distraction Blocking: Website blockers during work
  • Emergency Kit: Water, snacks, charger within reach

🌊 Achieving Transcription Flow State

Pre-work ritual (5-10 minutes):

  • Same workspace setup routine
  • Specific music or background sounds
  • Brief platform guidelines review
  • Clear desk of all distractions

Maintaining flow:

  • Start with easier files to build momentum
  • Keep essentials within reach
  • Use consistent keyboard shortcuts
  • Take micro-breaks between files (2-3 minutes)

When flow breaks:

  • Don't force it - take a real break
  • Switch to different work (admin, research)
  • Save difficult files for high-energy periods
  • Know when to stop for the day
