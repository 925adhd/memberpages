How to Start Working Online

Get ready to work on real websites and make money

⏰ Total Time: 60-90 minutes to get completely ready to work

🚀 You're Getting Ready to Work!

This guide helps you get ready to work on websites that pay you real money. Follow these steps to start your new job!

Step 1: Get Work Email
🎯 What You're Preparing For

You're getting ready to work on websites where companies pay you to:

  • Write things for them
  • Take surveys and give opinions
  • Test their websites
  • Type information
  • Help with small tasks

This is real work that pays real money!

📧 Step 1: Get Work Email (5 minutes)

💡 Why you need a work email

You need a separate email just for work. This keeps your work stuff separate from your personal stuff and makes everything easier!

1
Pick Your Email Provider

Easy option: Use Gmail, Yahoo, or any free email service

Extra secure option: Use ProtonMail.com if you want extra privacy

2
Pick a Good Name

Use your real name like: john.smith.work@gmail.com

Or: john.smith@protonmail.com

Don't use: coolguy123@gmail.com

3
Make a Password

Use a strong password. Write it down somewhere safe!

💰 This is FREE!

All these email services are free. You can use any of them to apply for jobs right away!

💡 Why a separate email helps

Having a work-only email means:

  • All your work messages stay in one place
  • You won't miss important job emails
  • You look more professional to companies
  • You can keep work and personal life separate

💳 Step 2: Set Up PayPal to Get Paid (10 minutes)

💡 Why you need PayPal

PayPal is how most websites pay you. After you finish work, they send money to your PayPal account!

1
Go to PayPal

Go to PayPal.com/business

2
Pick Business Account

Choose "Business Account" even if you work alone

3
Use Your New Email

Use the ProtonMail email you just made

💰 Extra Money Tip!

Get the PayPal debit card. It gives you money back when you buy things!

📱 Step 4: Make Money With Old Phones (10 minutes)

💡 What is this?

While you're working on other jobs, old phones can make extra money! They work all day and night making you money.

1
Find Old Devices

Look for old phones, tablets, or computers you don't use anymore

2
Download Honeygain

Go to Honeygain.com and download the app

3
Install and Run

Put the app on your device and let it run. Keep the device plugged in!

💰 How Much Money?

Each device makes about $1-3 per month. It's not much, but it's extra money while you do other work!

⚠️ Things to Know

This uses your internet. Make sure you have unlimited internet or it might cost you money!

✨ Step 5: Make a Good Profile to Get Hired (20 minutes)

💡 Why this matters

Your profile is like a job application. A good profile helps companies want to hire you for work!

1
Get a Good Photo
  • Use a clear photo of your face
  • Smile and look friendly
  • Wear nice clothes
  • Don't use selfies or group photos
2
Write a Good Job Title

Examples:

  • "Data Entry Expert - Fast and Accurate"
  • "Writer - I Help You Tell Your Story"
  • "Website Helper - I Make Sites Look Good"
  • "Survey Taker - I Give Good Opinions"
3
Tell People What Work You Do

Write 2-3 sentences about:

  • What kind of work you want to do
  • Why you're good at it
  • How you help companies
4
Apply for Your First Jobs

Now you're ready to apply for work! Look for:

  • Easy jobs that don't need much experience
  • Jobs that pay what you want
  • Jobs you can do from home
