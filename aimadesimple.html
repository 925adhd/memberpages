How to Get Started

Your first steps with AI

1
Pick One Tool
Start with ChatGPT or Claude. Both are free and easy to use. Don't try everything at once.
2
Make an Account
Sign up with your email. Most tools just need an email and password. It takes 2 minutes.
3
Ask Simple Questions
Start with easy stuff like "What's the weather like?" or "Help me write an email." Get comfortable first.
4
Try Different Things
Once you're comfortable, try asking for help with work, creative projects, or learning new things.
Remember: AI is just a tool - like a really smart calculator for words and ideas

Smart Tips for Using AI

Get better results with these tricks

üó£Ô∏è
Be Specific
Instead of "write something," try "write a friendly email to my boss asking for time off next Friday."
üîÑ
Ask for Changes
Don't like the first answer? Say "make it shorter" or "make it more casual" or "try again."
üé≠
Give It a Role
Say "act like a teacher" or "pretend you're a friend giving advice." It changes how AI responds.
üìù
Break Big Tasks Down
Instead of "help me with my resume," try "help me write a summary for my resume in marketing."
üéØ
Set the Context
Tell AI about your situation: "I'm a college student writing to a professor" helps it understand.
üß†
Use It for ADHD Help
Ask it to "break this project into small steps" or "help me organize my thoughts about this topic."
üö®
Important Things to Remember
  • AI can make mistakes - always double-check important stuff
  • Don't share personal info like passwords or Social Security numbers
  • AI doesn't know about very recent events (last few months)
  • It's a tool to help you, not replace your thinking
The best part: AI gets better the more you use it!
